PRIMARY SERVICES:

1. ENTERPRISE AI CHATBOTS
The company develops AI-powered chatbots designed for internal employees and external customers. These chatbots are trained using proprietary documents, FAQs, internal policies, and historical support data. They support contextual conversations, follow-up questions, and multilingual responses.

2. AI AGENT ORCHESTRATION
Altan Steppe builds AI agents capable of executing multi-step tasks such as data retrieval, document summarization, report generation, and API-based system interactions. These agents can autonomously reason through workflows using predefined tools and constraints.

3. VECTOR DATABASE IMPLEMENTATION
The company implements vector search solutions using Pinecone and similar databases to enable semantic search across large document collections. Typical use cases include internal knowledge bases, legal document retrieval, and customer support archives.

4. CUSTOM AI MODEL INTEGRATION
Clients can integrate third-party AI models such as OpenAI, Hugging Face, or locally hosted models. The company ensures secure API usage, monitoring, and fallback mechanisms.

5. WEB AND PLATFORM DEVELOPMENT
Using Next.js, React, and Node.js, the company builds secure dashboards and interfaces for managing AI agents, monitoring usage, and analyzing performance metrics.
